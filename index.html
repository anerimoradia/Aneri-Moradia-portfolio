Junior Software Engineer & Developer.

Hello, I'm Aneri Moradia, an enthusiastic junior software engineer. Technology has always fascinated me since my childhood, igniting a passion that has stayed with me throughout my journey. I vividly remember writing my very first code in 9th grade using HTML, an experience that propelled me to pursue a career in computer engineering.
As I embarked on this path, I discovered my innate desire to connect technology with the real world. It led me to develop a keen interest in photography, videography, and the art of picture editing. Exploring the synergy between technology and creativity has become an integral part of who I am.
My main aspiration is to become a well-rounded full-stack developer. With a solid foundation in both front-end and back-end development, I bring three years of valuable experience to the table. Java, C#, and Python are among the diverse skill set I have acquired, enabling me to tackle a range of projects with confidence.
Throughout my academic years, I engaged in numerous projects that showcased my proficiency in software development, machine learning, computer networking, and data visualization. These areas have become my expertise, fueling my drive to excel in the ever-evolving tech industry.
As I embark on the next chapter of my career, I am excited to contribute my skills, enthusiasm, and dedication to meaningful projects. I am eager to continue growing as a software engineer, making a lasting impact by leveraging technology to shape a better future.
